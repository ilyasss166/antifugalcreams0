How to Choose the Right Antifungal Cream

When selecting an antifungal cream, consider your infection type, skin sensitivity, and how quickly you need results. Products containing terbinafine, clotrimazole, or miconazole are clinically proven and available over the counter.

Key factors to look for:

  • Active ingredients (terbinafine, miconazole, etc.)
  • Application area (feet, groin, body)
  • Speed of relief and absorption
